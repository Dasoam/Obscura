PRIVACY NOTICE
--------------

By installing Obscura, you are installing a privacy-focused research browser
that operates with the following guarantees:

✓ ZERO TELEMETRY - No analytics or usage tracking
✓ ZERO DATA COLLECTION - Your searches and browsing are never logged
✓ ZERO CLOUD SYNC - Everything runs locally on your machine
✓ ZERO USER ACCOUNTS - No sign-in required, no identifiers created
✓ ZERO PERSISTENT COOKIES - Session data is cleared when you close the app

WHAT GETS STORED
----------------

The ONLY thing Obscura may store on your computer:
• UI Preferences (window size, last privacy mode selected)

This data is stored locally and is never transmitted anywhere.

THIRD-PARTY SERVICES
--------------------

Obscura may optionally connect to:
• SearxNG (local or public instance) - for search functionality
• Tor Network (if Tor mode enabled) - for anonymous browsing
• Websites you explicitly choose to visit

You control all network connections. Obscura never makes background
requests without your explicit action.
